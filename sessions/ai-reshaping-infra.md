# Engineering at Ludicrous Speed: How AI Is Reshaping Infra and Engineering

https://www.socallinuxexpo.org/scale/23x/presentations/engineering-ludicrous-speed-how-ai-reshaping-infra-and-engineering

- **Date**: Saturday, March 7, 2026
- **Time**: 17:00 - 18:00
- **Location**: Room 107
- **Speaker(s)**: James Bayer, VP Product, Flox; Stormy Peters, Head of Open Source Strategy, Amazon Web Services; Kelsey Hightower, Advisor, Flox; Ron Efroni, CEO & Co-Founder, Flox

> "This past year has been a whiplash for infrastructure and software teams. AI-native workflows are upending everything from provisioning to CI/CD, and open source communities are scrambling to catch up."
>
> The panel explores emerging patterns in how high-velocity engineering teams operate, including AI's impact on developer workflows, evolving infrastructure patterns, open source adaptation to agentic development, and lessons from building platforms at AWS, HashiCorp, and Flox. Attendees will learn what infrastructure and software development practices look like as these technologies reshape the industry.

## Overview

A panel of infrastructure and open-source leaders examines how AI-native workflows are transforming provisioning, CI/CD, and engineering culture at high-velocity teams, drawing on experience from AWS, HashiCorp, and Flox.
