# All Your Keyboards Are Belong To Us!

https://www.socallinuxexpo.org/scale/23x/presentations/all-your-keyboards-are-belong-us

- **Date**: Friday, March 6, 2026
- **Time**: 11:00 - 11:45
- **Location**: Ballroom C
- **Speaker(s)**: Federico Lucifredi, Product Management Director, IBM

> This live tutorial examines attack methodologies targeting keyboards across multiple forms. The presenter explores "the ultimate strategy to hijack a session before it is encrypted, capturing plaintext at the source."
>
> The session progression covers traditional keyloggers, "Van Eck Phreaking" style attacks on keystroke emanations and RF wireless connections, and acoustic attacks exploiting typing sounds.
>
> The presenter emphasizes ethical application: "Use your newfound knowledge for good, with great power comes great responsibility!" The content focuses on signal leak attacks using open-source materials, excluding classified information.

## Overview

A live tutorial surveying keyboard attack methodologies—from classic keyloggers to Van Eck Phreaking RF side-channels and acoustic keystroke inference—to illustrate how plaintext can be captured before it ever reaches encryption.
